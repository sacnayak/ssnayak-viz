About the dataset

Overview

The data set informs us of the spate of gun violence incidents in the country. This analysis is inspired by a series of Slate.com articles targeting the same.

Data Source

The data is obtained from the slate.com website. I created a readable fusion table for the same here.

For Byte 4

I wanted to try and use data as a compelling way to create a narrative describing the urgency and need for further gun control policies in the United States. My main D3-visualization is inspired form this tutorial. I imagined that a blood-red calendar throughout the year would make for a compelling narrative. It did turn out 'red-der' than a expected. Hope the calendar transitions to a few whites now on.

Inferences

It would be unfair to say things haven't improved since Newtown (since I got only the 2013 dataset). But 2013 certainly wasn't a huge improvement since the unfortunate incident. It'll be interesting to visualize the subsequent years till 2016.
